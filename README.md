### About

I build quantitative tools at the intersection of healthcare, finance, and machine learning. My work focuses on making M&A intelligence, health equity analytics, and social impact modeling more accessible through interactive visualization and open-source software.

Currently exploring late-stage M&A investing in healthtech and seeking AI fellowships to deepen applied ML in healthcare deal analysis.
